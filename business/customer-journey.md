 # Customer-Journey.md

## Aware

### Trigger Event
- Developer or QA engineer encounters a new project that requires testing for web and mobile apps.
- Team faces challenges in setting up testing infrastructure or maintaining existing solutions.

### Friction Points
- Lack of awareness about the existence of automated testing solutions.
- Difficulty in understanding the benefits of using an automated testing platform.
- Concerns about the complexity of setting up and maintaining the testing infrastructure.

### User Emotions
- Frustration due to time-consuming manual testing processes.
- Anxiety about the potential for errors and bugs in the final product.
- Skepticism towards new solutions due to past negative experiences.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Clear and concise messaging about the benefits of using an automated testing platform.
- Demonstration of the ease of setup and maintenance of the testing infrastructure.
- Success stories and testimonials from other developers or QA engineers who have used the product.

### Metric
- Number of new visitors to the product website or landing page.

## Consider

### Trigger Event
- Developer or QA engineer researches automated testing solutions to address their pain points.

### Friction Points
- Overwhelming number of options in the market.
- Difficulty in comparing features and pricing of different solutions.
- Lack of trust in new solutions due to potential hidden costs or complex licensing.

### User Emotions
- Curiosity about the potential benefits of automated testing.
- Caution due to the perceived complexity and cost of implementing a new solution.
- Confusion about the best option for their specific needs.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Clear and easy-to-understand comparison of features and pricing.
- Transparent pricing and licensing information.
- Demonstration of the ease of use and integration with existing development workflows.

### Metric
- Number of page views on the product comparison page.

## Try

### Trigger Event
- Developer or QA engineer decides to try the test-orchestra platform.

### Friction Points
- Difficulty in setting up the platform due to complex instructions or lack of documentation.
- Technical issues during the setup or testing process.
- Difficulty in understanding the results and troubleshooting any issues.

### User Emotions
- Excitement about the potential benefits of automated testing.
- Frustration due to setup or technical issues.
- Dissatisfaction if the results do not meet their expectations.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Clear and concise setup instructions.
- Responsive and helpful customer support.
- Easy-to-understand results and troubleshooting guidance.

### Metric
- Number of trial sign-ups or downloads.
- Number of support tickets or inquiries.

## Adopt

### Trigger Event
- Developer or QA engineer decides to adopt the test-orchestra platform as their primary testing solution.

### Friction Points
- Difficulty in integrating the platform with existing development workflows.
- Technical issues during the adoption process.
- Difficulty in training team members to use the platform.

### User Emotions
- Relief at finding a solution to their testing pain points.
- Satisfaction with the results and ease of use.
- Confidence in the platform's ability to improve their development process.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Clear and concise integration instructions.
- Ongoing support and training resources.
- Success stories and testimonials from other developers or QA engineers who have successfully adopted the platform.

### Metric
- Number of active users or subscribers.
- Number of support tickets or inquiries related to adoption.

## Expand

### Trigger Event
- Developer or QA engineer expands the use of the test-orchestra platform to additional projects or team members.

### Friction Points
- Difficulty in scaling the platform to accommodate larger teams or projects.
- Technical issues during the expansion process.
- Difficulty in managing and organizing tests across multiple projects.

### User Emotions
- Pride in the success of their projects and the role the test-orchestra platform played in that success.
- Satisfaction with the ease of use and scalability of the platform.
- Confidence in the platform's ability to continue supporting their development needs.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Scalable and flexible platform that can accommodate larger teams or projects.
- Ongoing support and training resources to help manage and organize tests across multiple projects.
- Success stories and testimonials from other developers or QA engineers who have successfully expanded their use of the platform.

### Metric
- Number of active projects using the test-orchestra platform.
- Number of team members using the platform.
