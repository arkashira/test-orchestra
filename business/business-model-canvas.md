## Customer Segments
* Web and mobile app developers, focusing on small to medium-sized businesses and startups
* Quality Assurance (QA) teams in larger enterprises looking to streamline their testing processes
* DevOps teams seeking to integrate automated testing into their Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment (CI/CD) pipelines

## Value Propositions
* Fast setup and low maintenance automated testing platform
* Reliable and accurate test results, reducing the risk of bugs and errors in production
* Support for both web and mobile apps, with a focus on ease of use and simplicity

## Channels
* Online marketing and advertising, targeting developer communities and QA teams
* Partnerships with popular development platforms and tools, such as GitHub and Jenkins
* Word-of-mouth referrals and case studies from satisfied customers

## Customer Relationships
* Personalized support and onboarding process for new customers
* Regular updates and feature releases based on customer feedback and suggestions
* Community-driven forums and documentation for users to share knowledge and best practices

## Revenue Streams
* Subscription-based model, with plans starting at $49/month (approximately 1,500 THB) for small teams and $199/month (approximately 6,000 THB) for larger enterprises
* Custom pricing for large-scale deployments and enterprise customers
* Additional revenue streams from add-on features and services, such as test data analytics and consulting

## Key Resources
* Development team with expertise in automated testing and DevOps
* Cloud infrastructure and scalable architecture to support large volumes of tests
* Partnerships with key technology providers and industry leaders

## Key Activities
* Continuous development and improvement of the testing platform
* Marketing and outreach efforts to build brand awareness and attract new customers
* Building and maintaining strategic partnerships with key industry players

## Key Partners
* GitHub and other development platforms for integration and collaboration
* Testing and QA tool providers for complementary solutions and joint marketing efforts
* Industry leaders and thought leaders for guidance and validation

* Development and maintenance costs, estimated at $100,000/month (approximately 3,000,000 THB)
